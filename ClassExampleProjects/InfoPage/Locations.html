4 Privet Drive

Harry's childhood home, where he lives with his aunt, uncle, and cousin.

Diagon Alley

A portion of London which consists of magical shops.

Hogwarts

School for witchcraft and wizardry, where Dumbledore is headmaster.

The Burrow

The home of the Weasley family.

12 Grimmauld Place

The home of Harry's godfather, Sirius Black, and the headquarters for the Order of the Phoenix.

Sorcerer's Stone

Meet Harry Potter, an 11 year old boy, who is famous for defeated the darkest wizard of the modern age.

Chamber of Secrets

Harry is now 12 years old and must take on and defeat an unknown monster who is attacking his classmates.

Prisoner of Azkaban

Harry, now a teenager, is given some news about his unknown godfather, and must decide how to treat him.

Goblet of Fire

Harry faces off against 3 other contestents in a tournament, but more than a trophy is at stake as strange things continue to happen.

Order of the Phoenix

With the return of Voldemort, resistence to him regaining power begins.

Half-Blood Prince

As Voldemort gains power in secret, Harry struggles through Advanced Potions with the help of hand-written notes in his book.

Deathly Hallows

The Secong Wizarding War is in full force as Harry, Hermione, and (sometimes) Ron wander around the countryside looking for parts of Voldemort's soul to destroy.

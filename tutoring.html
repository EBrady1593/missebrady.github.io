About Me

I grew up in Exeter Township, and still live there. I am a middle school computer science teacher in Lancaster County.

I have always loved helping others. I started tutoring in 10th grade. I began by tutoring other students in my school, through the guidance office, who were struggling in math.

Since then, I have helped a number of different people. Some of the people I have met with have been one time meetings, while others take place on a regular basis.

In college I had the opportunity to tutor elementary students in an after school program. I also worked with two high school students on a weekly basis, by recommendation of the faculty.

Since graduating in 2015, I have helped adults to gain computer skills and confidence with new technology, I have taught multiple summer camps, and I have continued to tutor high school students in different areas of mathematics.
